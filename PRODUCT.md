# Lacuna

> The void is the point.

## What it is

Lacuna is a social app for creative expression that mainstream feeds have squeezed out. The name is the diagnosis — *lacuna* means a gap, a missing piece. Conventional social media has produced a creativity vacuum by rewarding scripted, over-edited, algorithmically optimized performance. Lacuna fills that gap.

Posts live inside **albums** — user-defined collections that share a theme. The community votes on whether a post *fits the album* (upvote) or *doesn't* (downvote). The signal is curatorial, not popularity.

## Register

**Product.** Lacuna IS the experience, not a marketing wrapper around one. Every design decision serves the act of posting and consuming, not the act of selling Lacuna.

## Users

A person tired of being fed scripted content and overedited media on Instagram. Someone who wants something more **down to earth, expressive, and in the moment**. Most likely teens through adults — broad age range; the unifying trait is creative restlessness.

They are not:
- Influencer-economy participants chasing reach
- TikTok-native viewers conditioned to short-form algorithmic feeds
- Casual lurkers — Lacuna rewards posting and curating

They probably also use: Pinterest (for inspiration boards), Snapchat (for raw friend-only posts), film cameras, journaling apps, BeReal-adjacent tools.

## Promise

> Lacuna is for people who want to express their creative vision without being lost in a sea of scripted and overhyped content creators.

## Tone

**raw · cozy · nostalgic**

- **Raw** — unfiltered. Grain, texture, imperfect crops. Things that look like they happened, not things that look like they were produced.
- **Cozy** — warmth, dim ambient light, the feeling of being at home with friends rather than performing on a stage. Not slick. Not corporate.
- **Nostalgic** — references to a pre-algorithmic era of social media. Snapchat-OG energy: ephemeral, friend-scale, low-stakes.

Opening Lacuna should produce **curiosity**. Not the dopamine-pull of an infinite feed, but the way you feel flipping through a friend's polaroid box.

## Anti-references

| App | What to avoid |
|---|---|
| **Instagram** | Glossy. Saturated. Influencer-coded. Stories format. The grid. Pristine product photography aesthetic. The blue-purple gradient. |
| **TikTok** | Loud. Algorithmically aggressive. Vertical video supremacy. The "For You" pull. Auto-play with sound. Watermarks. |
| **Twitter/X** | Discourse. Quote-tweet pile-ons. Reply hierarchies. Public square energy. |
| **BeReal** | Forced timing gimmick. Black-and-white minimalism. The notification-driven loop. |

Lacuna is not a Snapchat clone either — Snapchat's *original* spirit is a starting reference, not a copy target.

## Strategic principles

- **Albums over algorithms.** The unit of curation is the album theme, not the global feed rank.
- **Curatorial voting.** Up/downvote reflects *fit*, not approval. The verdict is "does this belong here?" not "do I like this?"
- **Friend-scale by default.** Following relationships and friend graphs matter more than viral reach. Public/private should default toward the smaller circle.
- **Real over polished.** When in doubt, lean into texture, grain, hand-feel. Slick is the enemy.
- **No infinite scroll attention traps.** Sessions should feel like a deliberate visit, not a slot machine.
- **The void is the point.** Resist the temptation to fill every gap. Whitespace, quiet, and rest are part of the brand.

## Surfaces in scope

Mobile-first across phone / tablet / foldable / desktop / web. Both portrait and landscape supported — never lock orientation. Light and dark themes both, system-followed by default.

## Glossary

| Term | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Album** | A user-defined themed collection. The unit posts belong to. (Internal/legacy code may use `blob` — that's outdated dev jargon; the user-facing term is **album**.) |
| **Verdict** | The aggregate up/downvote on a post within its album. |
| **Trace** | An entry in activity history. |
| **Friend or Foe** | A two-player game inside Lacuna for testing relationship vibes. |

`Anucal` is a scrapped concept — remove any remaining references.
